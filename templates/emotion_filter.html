Emotion Analysis Scoring Guide
How Scoring Works

Our emotion analysis uses advanced NLP to classify articles into 8 emotion categories based on language patterns, keywords, and sentiment analysis.

Scoring Process
  1. Text Processing: Articles are cleaned and tokenized
  2. Keyword Analysis: Emotion-specific keywords are identified
  3. Sentiment Scoring: Overall sentiment is calculated (-1.0 to +1.0)
  4. Emotion Classification: Articles are classified into emotion categories
  5. Confidence Scoring: Each classification gets a confidence percentage
